The wizard Elion

Description

When the old wizard Elion tried to conjure a sphere of light, a mistake transported him to a modern shopping mall full of monsters. Now, lost among shop windows and strange creatures, Elion needs to find a way to correct his spell and return to his kingdom, before the chaos spreads further and the monsters are completely released. ---------The game has 3 languages: Portuguese, English and Italian ---------You can play on your mobile device too. Good fun!
