Wall Ball Wizard

Description

In this fast-paced, action-packed game, you control a wall piece that must bounce a ball back and forth to keep it from falling off the screen. The longer you can keep the ball in play, the higher your score. The game gets faster and more challenging as you progress, so youll need to be quick and nimble to keep up.
